How it works


Welcome to U-Medic, the platform that connects you with expert physicians anytime, anywhere. Now, are you confused on what to do next and how the platform really works? Relax, I will explain all you need to know here, so
follow the simple steps below:

Download the app


The U-Medic application is readily available on Google Play
and iOS app store.

Sign Up


After you must have downloaded the app, the next thing to do is to sign up with your personal information. Do not worry, your personal information are safe with us and we will not under any illegal conditions divulge it to third parties without your permission.

Subscription


To use U-Medic services, you are required to make specific payments depending on the nature of the service to be provided.
